LEARN TO PLAY SALSA MUSIC PERCUSSION

What You'll Learn

Music

Understand the theory and structure of salsa music, along with the different types percusion rhythms and how they are applied in the music.

Congas

The main instrument that is learned. Practicing techniques, timing, and learning how to play with music.

Claves

Know how to play the son clave and the rumba clave rythms and how different instruments play to the clave.

Cowbell

Also, known as campana. Learn the basic cowbell pattern along with the variations.

Timbales

Practice the cascara rhythm, along with understanding the application of the timbales.

Bongos

Learn the basic bongo pattern and the variations. Practice the technique of the hits.

Guiro

Practice the guiro patterns. Play with other instruments and learn when the guiro is used.

We learn from the greats, such as Changuito, Giovanni Hidalgo, Ray Barretto, Poncho Sanchez, and Joe Cuba. We watch You Tube videos of salsa percusionists along with develop our ears by listening deeply to salsa music.

We post instructional videos as well, that we learned in class. This way we can practice the techniques at home. All this is posted in the Soul Drummers Facebook Group. Join the group to see the catalogue of videos we watch.

About

Soul Drummers was started in Los Angeles in 2018 by Abraham Rosales and Michelle Ramos, two tenured salsa dancers from Bonito y Sabroso. The goal of Soul Drummers is teach salsa music percussion, with the side benifit of becoming better salsa dancers.

On Wednesday nights, we spend 2 hours going over techniques, rythms, salsa percussion theory. This is where we get hands on instruction and feedback from Sensei Abraham Rosales.

Weekly, instructional and non-instructional videos are posted on the Soul Drummers Facebook Group. This where we can get inspired and continue to learn from more experienced players.

Inbetween classes, students are expected to practice rythms and techniques learned class. This is where students improve the most.
